Privacy Policy

Product: LinkedIn Post Generator (Chrome extension)

Contact: devopsviji02@gmail.com

What we do

The extension helps you draft a LinkedIn post and, if you choose, inserts it into the LinkedIn composer.

Data we process

  • User-provided text (your prompt/topic and options) is sent to OpenAI solely to generate the requested post. We do not receive or store this content.
  • Telemetry (event names like “generate_ok”) is sent to Google Analytics with a random client ID to measure reliability. No page content or personal data is included.
  • Local storage only: your OpenAI API key, license status, usage counters, and the random analytics client ID are stored in chrome.storage.local on your device. They are not synced to the developer.

What we do not collect

No names, emails, payment data, browsing history, keystrokes, or personal communications are collected.

Sharing

Data is shared only with service providers necessary to deliver the feature: OpenAI (content generation) and Google Analytics (anonymous telemetry). We do not sell data or use it for advertising or credit decisions.

Your choices

  • You can clear the extension’s storage at any time in Chrome.
  • You can opt out of telemetry by uninstalling the extension.
